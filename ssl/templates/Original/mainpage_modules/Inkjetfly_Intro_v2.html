Inkjetfly Continuous Ink Supply System (CIS/CISS)

Inkjetfly G5 Continuous Ink Supply System (CISS) is easy, provides high quality prints, and can save your business 90% of its printing costs!

Inkjetfly G5 offers non-interrupt ink supply without ever having to change ink cartridges. The system requires no permanent modification to the printer and will not void your printer's warranty.

The high quality inks provide vivid prints and a clog-free print head. Unlike other ink supply system, which may have serious color problems or fade over time, Inkjetfly G5 inks are guaranteed to meet or exceed the original equipment manufacturer's ink.
