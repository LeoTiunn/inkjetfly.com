InkjetFly Photo Black V3 Pigment Ink is Available Now!
InkjetFly V3 Photo Black Pigment Ink(IJFV3PK) is available now for R1800, R1900, R2400, R2880, Pro3800, 1400 Ink Sets. The V3 PK provides 3 times blackness over original IS PK - L*a*b 5.8(IJFV3PK) vs 19.2(ISPK), also provides virtually indentical performnace as OEM PK ( L*a*b 3.4 ) in terms of blackness.

With the dense blacks in V3 PK , you will not only get greater dimension to overall image but also improved glossiness nearly rivals that of OEM.

Starting from 2/23/2009, we will use IJFV3PK on R1900, R1800 CIS BY DEFAULT. The ink bottle customers received will clearly show IJFV3PK label on it.

It is optional for R2400, R2880, 1400, Pro3800 CIS/Refillable Cartridges, customer can choose either ISPK (lower price) or IJFV3PK under "Available Options:".

For exsting customers, you are welcome to purchase new IJFV3PK from Bulk Ink Refill section.

New free custom R1900, R1800 printer profiles for V3 PK has been added into our profile database!!
