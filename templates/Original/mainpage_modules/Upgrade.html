Upgrade Information for Inkjetfly CIS

As you may know, Inkjetfly G5 Continuous Ink Systems(CIS) are going to be replaced with G6 Continuous Ink Supply Systems by 31st July, 2007. All G5 CIS customers will be eligible for a free upgrade to G6 CIS with a purchase of a full set of refill.

** This is the best time to purchase Inkjetfly CIS. If you purchase a $99.95 of R800/R1800 G5 CIS right now, you will qualify for a free upgrade to a $179.95 of R800/R1800 G6 CIS. It's a $80 saving.
