• Continuous Ink Supply System (CISS) offers non-interrupted ink supply without changing ink cartridges. The Inkjetfly G5 CISS simply attaches to the Epson/Canon Inkjet printer to provide automatic refilling of the printer cartridges. This system offers continuous printing without frequently replace cartridges for a significant cost savings. CISS is also known as Continuous Flow System (CFS), Continuous Ink System (CIS), or Continuous Inking System, Bulk Feed Inkjet System and Bulk Ink System with different vendors.

  • Compared to other similar products on the US market, Inkjetfly G5 Continuous Ink Supply System is two generations ahead. Its unique design eases ink replenishment. The high quality inks provide vivid colors and clog-free print head. CISS can be installed in minutes at home, which can save your time, money, and wear and tear on your printers. CISS also does not need any permanent modification to the printer, which means no voiding in warranties.
  • You don’t have to purchase the disposable ink cartridge frequently, and it does not require any contacts to the printer head. The CISS prevents dust from entering the ink print head and extends its service life. Its large ink capacity helps businesses to reduce printing costs more than 90%, while the high quality ink produces accurate colors and does not clog print heads.
